Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 10, 2025

1. Overview

License Plate Tracker (“the Service”) collects and displays sightings of official government vehicles for transparency and public-interest purposes. We respect your privacy and collect only the data necessary to operate the platform.

2. Data We Collect

  • License plate identifiers of official government vehicles
  • Approximate location and timestamp of sightings
  • Optional metadata such as vehicle type or notes
  • Standard web server logs (e.g., IP address, browser type) for security, debugging, and abuse prevention.

3. How We Use Data

Data is used solely to display sightings, ensure proper functioning of the Service, and prevent abuse. We do not attempt to identify, contact, or associate license plates with private individuals or vehicle owners.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data. Data may be disclosed only if required by law (e.g., valid subpoena or court order).

5. Data Retention and Security

Sightings and metadata are stored securely and retained only as long as necessary for the Service to function. We apply standard encryption and access controls.

6. Cookies

The Service may use basic cookies or local storage to remember preferences or improve usability. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

License Plate Tracker operates with a minimal-data approach and avoids personal identification. Use of the Service signifies agreement with this Privacy Policy.
